Jaipur

Jaipur, the Pink City

Jaipur is the capital and the largest city of the Indian state of Rajasthan. It was founded on 18th November 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, who ruled the state from 1699 to 1743. The city is known for its architecture, art, and culture. It is also known as the Pink City because of the colour of the stone used in the buildings of the old city.

Jaipur is a major tourist destination in India. It attracts millions of tourists every year. The city is famous for its forts, palaces, and monuments. Some of the popular tourist attractions in Jaipur include the Amber Fort, the City Palace, the Jantar Mantar, the Hawa Mahal, and the Jal Mahal.

Jaipur is also known for its vibrant culture and traditions. The city is famous for its handicrafts, textiles, and jewelry. The city is also known for its delicious cuisine, which includes dishes like dal bati churma, laal maans, and g hewar.
